Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Shipping Areas

Hexa Computronics offers delivery services across **Kanpur** and pan-India. For orders within Kanpur city, we provide same-day or next-day express delivery.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on the weight of the product and the delivery location. Most of our premium refurbished laptops qualify for **Free Shipping** as indicated on the product page. For other items, shipping costs are clearly mentioned during checkout.

3. Delivery Timeline

  • Local (Kanpur): 24 to 48 working hours.
  • Uttar Pradesh: 3 to 5 working days.
  • Rest of India: 5 to 7 working days.

4. Packaging & Safety

We use industry-standard, multi-layer shockproof packaging to ensure your refurbished laptop or desktop reaches you in perfect condition. Every shipment is insured against transit damage.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via WhatsApp or Email. You can also contact our support at **9935529306** for real-time updates.
